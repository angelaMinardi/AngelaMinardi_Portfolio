Analyzing Reading Proficiency Trends Using NAEP Data

In this project, I analyzed reading proficiency trends among students using datasets from the National Assessment of Educational Progress (NAEP). The analysis focuses on understanding how reading scores have evolved over time and across different demographic groups. from scipy.stats import f_oneway

# Function to perform ANOVA
def perform_anova(group_scores):
    return f_oneway(*group_scores.values())

Insights and Recommendations

  • Trend Analysis: Both datasets show an upward trend in average reading scores over the years, indicating improvement in reading proficiency.
  • Demographic Disparities: Significant differences in reading scores among demographic groups were observed, highlighting the need for targeted support.
  • Targeted Interventions: Focus on demographic groups with lower reading scores to provide tailored support and resources.
  • Continuous Monitoring: Implement real-time data tracking to monitor student progress and adjust strategies promptly.
  • Resource Allocation: Allocate more resources to groups that demonstrate slower progress to bridge the achievement gap.
  • Further Research: Conduct qualitative studies to understand underlying factors affecting reading proficiency in different demographic groups.
